What is an Investment Plan?

A roadmap that guides your financial decisions to help achieve your long-term monetary goals

Strategic Financial Roadmap

A structured approach to allocating your resources across various investment vehicles based on your goals and risk profile.

Wealth Multiplier

Harnesses the power of compounding and systematic investing to grow your money exponentially over time.

Protects your future with diversification and goal-based allocation, minimizing risks while maximizing returns.

How to Create a Proper Investment Plan

Follow these six essential steps to build a comprehensive investment strategy tailored to your unique situation

1

Goal Setting

Define clear, measurable financial objectives with specific timelines. Whether it's retirement, education, or buying a home.

Example Goal:

"Save $100,000 for a down payment on a house within 5 years"

2

Risk Profiling

Assess your comfort level with market fluctuations and potential losses to determine your risk tolerance.

Risk Profiles:

Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive

3

Time Horizon

Determine how long you plan to keep your money invested before you need to access it.

Horizons:

Short-term (< 3 years), Medium (3-10 years), Long-term (10+ years)

4

Asset Allocation

Distribute your investments across various asset classes to balance risk and potential returns.

Asset Classes:

Equity, Debt, Real Estate, Gold, Cash

5

Investment Selection

Choose specific investment products within each asset class that align with your goals and risk profile.

Examples:

Index funds, Blue-chip stocks, Government bonds

6

Monitoring & Rebalancing

Regularly review your portfolio's performance and make adjustments to maintain your target allocation.

Frequency:

Quarterly review, Annual rebalancing
